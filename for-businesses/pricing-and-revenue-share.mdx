Kash organizations are free to start. You can create your organization, curate markets, and begin earning revenue share without paying anything up front. Paid tiers add capability as you grow.

<Note>
The current tier program is subject to change. For live plan details, see the Partner Portal pricing page, or contact [partnerships@kash.bot](mailto:partnerships@kash.bot).
</Note>

## How the model works

- **Free to start.** Creating an organization and getting your first markets live costs nothing beyond per-market creation fees.
- **Revenue share scales with your trading volume.** The more trading activity your markets generate, the larger the share of fees you keep. Higher subscription tiers also carry higher shares.
- **Higher tiers unlock more capability** — custom branding across your pages and embeds, dedicated infrastructure, and org-scoped API credentials.
- **Community Intelligence** — a premium analytics add-on with deeper audience insight and data export.

## How revenue share is earned

You earn a share of the trading fees on **every trade in markets your organization creates** — attribution is at the trade level, tied to the market's creator.

<Info>
It doesn't matter where the trade happens. Whether a user trades on app.kash.bot, through an embed on someone else's site, or from X, a trade in your market earns you your share. Embed `?source=` tags are analytics-only — they don't determine who gets paid. See [API access](/for-businesses/api-access#embed-tracking-is-analytics-only).
</Info>

### From trade to payout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Trades happen">
    Users trade in markets your organization created — on the webapp, in embeds, or via X.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earnings accrue automatically">
    Your share of each trade's fees is recorded as it happens. You can watch it accumulate in the portal's [earnings view](/for-businesses/analytics-and-earnings).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payouts settle periodically">
    Accrued earnings are paid out periodically after a settlement hold. Settled payouts appear as revenue events in the portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>
