Everything your organization generates — trading activity, audience growth, revenue — is measured and visible in the Partner Portal.

## Daily stats

The portal's **Analytics** section tracks your organization day by day:

| Metric | What it measures |
|---|---|
| Volume | USDC trading volume across your markets |
| Trades | Number of trades executed in your markets |
| Unique traders | Distinct users who traded in your markets |
| Active markets | Markets of yours open for trading |
| New users | Users who joined through your markets |

Use these to see which markets, threads, and campaigns actually move your audience — and drill into individual markets for a closer look.

## Insights

The **Insights** section goes deeper than the daily counters, with audience and market intelligence for your organization.

## Earnings and revenue events

The **Earnings** view shows the money side:

- **Accrued revenue share** — your share of trading fees builds up automatically as trades happen in markets your organization created. See [Pricing and revenue share](/for-businesses/pricing-and-revenue-share) for how attribution works.
- **Revenue events** — a ledger of everything that affects your balance: trading fees, platform fees, subscription charges, payouts, refunds, and settled revenue share.
- **Settled payouts** — accrued earnings are paid out periodically after a settlement hold, and each settlement appears as its own revenue event.

## Per-partner attribution

Revenue and activity are attributed to the partner responsible, so you can see which of your curators and creators drive volume — useful for deciding who to invest in, and for structuring your own revenue splits with partners.

## Data export

Organizations on the premium **Community Intelligence** analytics add-on can export their data for use in their own BI and reporting tools.
