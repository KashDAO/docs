The Partner Portal is the self-serve home for your organization: analytics, earnings, market curation, branding, team, and billing. This page walks you from first sign-in to a fully set-up organization.

## 1. Sign in

The portal uses Privy for authentication. Sign in with:

- **Email** — a one-time code, no password
- **Google**
- **X (Twitter)**

Signing in with X is a natural fit if you plan to run markets for your X audience, but any method works.

## 2. Create your organization

On first sign-in you'll be prompted to create your organization:

| Field | What it's for |
|---|---|
| Name | Your organization's name |
| Slug | Your URL handle — this becomes your public namespace on app.kash.bot |
| Contact email | Where Kash reaches you about your organization |
| Branding | Display name, tagline, and logo — all editable later |

New organizations start on the basic tier under the Kash parent organization. You can upgrade at any time from Billing.

## 3. Work through the onboarding checklist

The portal dashboard shows a setup checklist that tracks four steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Organization created">
    Done as soon as you complete step 2.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Billing active">
    Set up a paid subscription in **Billing** if you want tiered features — free-tier organizations can skip this and still curate markets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Platform connected">
    Connect the platform where your markets will be distributed — for example your X account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credentials stored">
    Store the delivery credentials for your connected platform so Kash can post and respond on your behalf.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 4. Find your way around

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Dashboard and analytics">
    Your landing page: setup progress, headline stats, and recent activity. **Analytics** breaks down volume, trades, unique traders, active markets, and new users over time — see [Analytics and earnings](/for-businesses/analytics-and-earnings).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Insights">
    Deeper audience and market intelligence for your organization.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Earnings">
    Your accrued revenue share and settled payouts, with per-partner attribution.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Fund">
    Manage your organization's smart account balance — used, for example, to pay market creation fees on behalf of your creators.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Markets">
    The heart of the portal: your community's suggestions, your proposal queue, and your live markets. See [Market creation](/for-businesses/market-creation).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Share">
    Embed codes and share links for your branded markets, threads, and organization grid.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Settings">
    Account, team members, partners, platform connections, revenue share, referral programs, and trade access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Billing">
    Your subscription tier and payment details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
