# How It Works

**Kash Kustom is designed for simplicity - you focus on your brand and community, we handle everything else.**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Twitter Account">
    **Use Your Existing Account or Create a New One**

    - Choose any Twitter account for your prediction markets
    - Could be your main brand account or a dedicated prediction account
    - Kash configures the account to process predictions
    - Your audience interacts only with YOUR brand, never @kash_bot
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch Your First Markets">
    **You and Your Community Create Markets**

    - Create markets tailored to your audience's interests
    - Your community can also suggest and create markets
    - Kash handles all market creation, pricing, and infrastructure
    - Markets go live within minutes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Engage Your Community">
    **Users Make Predictions on Your Brand**

    - Users quote tweet your account to place predictions
    - Your account automatically replies with confirmations
    - Natural language processing handles all prediction formats
    - Real-time odds updates keep users engaged
  </Step>

  <Step title="Earn Revenue & Grow">
    **Generate Value from Engagement**

    - Earn fees based on trading volume in your markets
    - Track engagement metrics and audience insights
    - Build competitive leaderboards and challenges
    - Scale your prediction offering as your community grows
  </Step>
</Steps>
