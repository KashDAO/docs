# Primary Platforms

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="X (Twitter) - Main Platform">
    Kash is fully embedded into X (formerly Twitter), bringing prediction markets directly to social media conversations.
    
    **@kash_bot Integration:**
    - **Reply to market posts** with your predictions using natural language
    - **DM @kash_bot** for private prediction and account management
    - **Tag @kash_bot** in conversations to create new markets
    - **Follow market updates** and notifications in your timeline
    
    **Where Markets Appear:**
    - In trending topic discussions
    - Celebrity and influencer threads
    - Breaking news conversations
    - Sports and entertainment events
    
    <Warning>
    Always verify you're interacting with the official @kash_bot account. Check the username carefully before placing any predictions.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Web Dashboard">
    For comprehensive account management, visit **kash.bot**:
    - **Wallet Management:** Fund your account and view balances
    - **Prediction History:** Complete transaction and prediction records
    - **Market Creation:** Advanced tools for proposing new markets
    - **Analytics:** Performance tracking and statistics
    - **Export Data:** Download records for tax and personal use
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Blockchain Infrastructure

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Base Network">
    **Built on Base (Coinbase L2):**
    - **High Performance:** Fast transaction processing
    - **Low Costs:** Minimal gas fees for all operations
    - **Ethereum Security:** Inherits Ethereum's proven security model
    - **EVM Compatible:** Full smart contract functionality
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Wallet Integration">
    **Non-Custodial Wallets:**
    - **Privy MPC Technology:** No seed phrases required
    - **Account Abstraction:** Gasless transactions via ERC-4337
    - **Recovery via X:** Restore access through your Twitter account
    - **Cross-Platform:** Same wallet across web and social media
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

# Device & Access

**Universal Compatibility:**

- **No Downloads:** Works entirely through existing platforms
- **Cross-Device Sync:** Your account follows you everywhere

# Global Availability

<Steps>
  <Step title="Worldwide Access">
    Kash operates globally with jurisdiction-specific compliance measures
  </Step>
  <Step title="Regulatory Compliance">
    U.S. users have limited functionality (max $10 predictions), and high-risk countries may be blocked at the frontend level
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decentralized Architecture">
    Built on public blockchain infrastructure to ensure censorship resistance
  </Step>
  <Step title="24/7 Operation">
    Markets and trading available around the clock across all time zones
  </Step>
</Steps>
