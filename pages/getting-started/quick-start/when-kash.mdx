# Perfect Moments for Prediction

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Breaking News">
    **Real-Time Market Creation**
    
    AI agents continuously monitor social signals and news feeds to create markets instantly when significant events unfold:
    - **Political Developments:** Election announcements, policy decisions
    - **Sports Events:** Game outcomes, championship results
    - **Financial News:** Earnings releases, market movements
    - **Technology:** Product launches, company announcements
    - **Entertainment:** Award shows, trending content
    
    **Advantage:** Be among the first to capitalize on new information
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Trending Discussions">
    **Social Media Integration**
    
    Markets appear naturally within X conversations:
    - **Trending Hashtags:** Join predictions on trending topics
    - **Influencer Threads:** Predict on outcomes discussed by thought leaders
    - **News Commentary:** Predict developments in breaking stories
    - **Community Debates:** Put your money where your opinion is
    
    **Advantage:** Predict while engaging in natural social media conversations
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Scheduled Events">
    **Planned Opportunities**
    
    Get ahead of known future events:
    - **Earnings Seasons:** Company financial results
    - **Sports Calendars:** Playoff games, championships
    - **Political Calendar:** Elections, policy announcements
    - **Product Launches:** Tech releases, automotive unveilings
    
    **Advantage:** Early positioning often offers better odds and higher potential returns
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

# Market Duration Framework

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ultra-Short Term (Minutes to Hours)">
    **Immediate Outcomes**
    - Live sports events and real-time developments
    - Instant market reactions to breaking news
    - Same-day political announcements
    - Cryptocurrency price movements within trading sessions
    
    **Characteristics:**
    - High volatility and rapid odds changes
    - Quick resolution and fast payouts
    - Multiple prediction opportunities per day
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Short Term (Days to Weeks)">
    **Near-Future Events**
    - Upcoming sports matches and tournaments
    - Earnings announcements and corporate events
    - Political developments and policy decisions
    - Technology product launches and reviews
    
    **Characteristics:**
    - More time for research and analysis
    - Odds evolution based on new information
    - Balanced risk-reward opportunities
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Maximum Duration (Up to 30 Days)">
    **Extended Timeframe Limit**
    - All Kash markets resolve within 30 days maximum
    - Ensures timely payouts and maintains engagement
    - Prevents indefinite market exposure
    - Keeps predictions relevant and actionable
    
    **Why 30 Days:**
    - Optimal balance between prediction value and resolution speed
    - Maintains liquidity and active participation
    - Enables comprehensive event coverage without excessive delay
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Optimal Trading Windows

<Steps>
  <Step title="Market Creation Phase">
    **Best for Early Adopters**
    - Markets appear as AI detects trending topics
    - Initial odds often favor informed early participants
    - Lower competition from other predictors
    - Opportunity to influence market sentiment
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Information Development">
    **Research and Analysis Period**
    - New information emerges and odds adjust
    - Multiple entry and exit opportunities
    - Time to gather additional insights
    - Market efficiency gradually improves
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pre-Resolution Window">
    **Final Positioning**
    - Last chance to participate before market closes
    - Most information available for decision-making
    - Reduced uncertainty but potentially lower returns
    - Quick resolution and payout timeline
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Resolution">
    **AI-Powered Settlement**
    - Markets close at predetermined times
    - AI systems verify outcomes using cryptographic proofs
    - Automatic distribution of winnings
    - Immediate availability of funds for new predictions
  </Step>
</Steps>

# Global Operation

**24/7 Market Availability:**
- **Continuous Monitoring:** AI systems track global events across all time zones
- **Always Active:** Markets available whenever you want to participate
- **Cross-Timezone Events:** Predict outcomes regardless of your location
- **Real-Time Updates:** Instant notifications and odds adjustments

**Peak Activity Periods:**
- **News Cycles:** Major announcements drive higher participation
- **Prime Time:** Regional peak hours see increased liquidity
- **Event Days:** Scheduled events create concentrated trading opportunities
- **Trending Moments:** Social media trends generate spontaneous market activity

<Tip>
The optimal time to use Kash is whenever you have conviction about a future outcome. AI ensures relevant markets are always available when events unfold or discussions develop.
</Tip>
