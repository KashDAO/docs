# Primary Currency

## USDC (USD Coin)
**The main currency for all predictions:**
- Stable value pegged to US Dollar
- Widely available on exchanges and wallets
- Low transaction fees on Base network
- Instant settlement for winnings

## Other Supported Tokens
- **ETH, WETH, DAI** - Auto-converted to USDC
- **$KASH tokens** - Can be used for predicting
- **Base network only** - Ensure correct network

# Deposit Methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Crypto Transfer">
    **Transfer crypto from exchanges or wallets**
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Get your Kash wallet address">
        - DM @kash_bot with "wallet" or "address"
        - Visit kash.bot dashboard
        - Copy your Base network address
        - Save for future use
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send USDC on Base network">
        - From any exchange or wallet that supports Base
        - **Critical:** Select Base network (not Ethereum!)
        - Send USDC to your Kash address
        - Arrives in 1-2 minutes
      </Step>
      <Step title="Other tokens auto-convert">
        - Send ETH, WETH, or DAI on Base
        - Automatically swapped to USDC at best rates
        - No manual conversion needed
        - Small conversion fee applies
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Warning>
    Always double-check you're using **Base network** - wrong network = lost funds!
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Card & Bank Payments">
    **Buy crypto directly with traditional payment methods**
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit kash.bot dashboard">
        - Click "Add Funds" or "Deposit"
        - Select amount ($10 minimum recommended)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose payment method">
        - Credit/debit card
        - Bank transfer (ACH)
        - Apple Pay/Google Pay
        - PayPal (where available)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete purchase">
        - Rates and fees shown upfront
        - USDC sent directly to your Kash wallet
        - Usually arrives in 5-10 minutes
        - Email confirmation sent
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Earn & Receive">
    **Get funds through Kash ecosystem activities**
    
    - **Win $KASH tokens** from competitions and challenges
    - **Receive tips** from other users for good predictions
    - **Earn referral rewards** for bringing new users
    - **Market creation rewards** for successful markets
    - **Convert $KASH** to prediction power automatically
    - **Community rewards** and airdrops
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

- **Start with small amounts** for your first deposit
- **Verify wallet addresses** carefully before sending
- **Save your Kash address** for future deposits
- **Enable X/Twitter notifications** for deposit confirmations
