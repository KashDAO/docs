# Notifications

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What types of notifications does Kash send?">
    **Notification Categories**
    
    **Predictions:**
    - **Prediction Win:** Confirmation when your prediction wins
    - **Prediction Loss:** Notification when your prediction loses
    
    **Leverage:**
    - **Position Confirmed:** When your leverage position is successfully opened
    - **Position Liquidated:** Alert when your leverage position is liquidated
    
    **Limit Orders:**
    - **Order Confirmed:** When your limit order is placed successfully
    - **Order Executed:** When your limit order is filled
    
    **Market Creation:**
    - **Market Accepted:** When your proposed market is accepted
    - **Market Rejected:** When your proposed market is rejected
    
    **Community:**
    - **Leaderboard Results:** Updates on leaderboard rankings
    - **Friend Tagged:** When friends tag you in discussions
    
    **Social (Web App):**
    - **Whale Activity:** See when whales you follow make trades
    - **KOL Trades:** See when KOLs you follow trade
    - **Profitable Trader Activity:** Track activity from profitable traders you follow

    All notifications include links to the relevant market or post.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Where can I receive notifications?">
    **Notification Delivery**

    **X Interface (Reply-Based):**
    - **@mention Replies:** When you @mention @kash_bot, confirmations and results are delivered as replies to your tweet
    - **DM Responses:** When you DM @kash_bot, responses are delivered within your DM conversation
    - @kash_bot only messages you in response to your interactions — no unsolicited DMs or mentions

    **Kash Web App:**
    - **In-App Notifications:** Notification center within the web application
    - **Links to Posts:** Direct links to the relevant X posts
    - **Social Activity Feeds:** Whale, KOL, and friend activity visible in the dashboard

    **Notification Categories Available:**
    - **All:** All notification types
    - **Predictions:** Prediction win/loss notifications
    - **Leverage:** Position confirmed and liquidated alerts
    - **Limit Orders:** Order confirmed and executed updates
    - **Market Creation:** Market accepted vs rejected updates
    - **Community:** Leaderboard results and friend tags
    - **Social:** Whale, KOL, and profitable trader activity (web app)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do notifications work with posts and messaging?">
    **Notification Features**

    **Links to Posts:**
    - **Direct Access:** Notifications include links to the relevant X post or market
    - **Context:** See the full conversation and market activity
    - **Engagement:** Interact directly with the post from the notification

    **How @kash_bot Communicates:**
    - **Reply-based:** @kash_bot replies to your tweets when you @mention it
    - **DM-based:** @kash_bot responds within DM conversations you initiate
    - **No unsolicited contact:** @kash_bot never DMs or @mentions you first

    **Both Platforms:**
    - **X Interface:** Receive responses from @kash_bot when you interact with it
    - **Web App:** View all notifications and activity in the Kash web application
    - **Consistent Experience:** Same information available across both platforms
    - **Real-time Updates:** Responses and market updates delivered promptly
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
@kash_bot only responds when you interact with it first — via @mention or DM. Always verify the authenticity of any messages and be cautious when clicking links from unknown sources.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Your primary interaction is on X — @kash_bot responds when you @mention or DM it. The companion dashboard at kash.bot provides additional activity feeds and analytics.
</Tip>
