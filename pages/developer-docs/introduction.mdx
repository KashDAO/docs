## What Can You Build?

### Embed Prediction Markets
Integrate live prediction markets into news sites, financial platforms, or community forums. Markets are read-only - users click through to Kash for transactions.

### Custom Dashboards
Fetch real-time market data programmatically to build custom analytics dashboards, trading interfaces, or data visualization tools.

### Content Enrichment
Enhance articles and posts with relevant prediction market probabilities to provide readers with real-time sentiment and forecasting data.
