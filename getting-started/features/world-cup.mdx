# Inside the hub

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Schedule and matches">
    The full fixture list, filterable by date, with live matches flagged as they happen. Every fixture has its own match page, and fixtures with a live Kash market link straight to it. Where no market is live yet, indicative projections from Kash's model are shown — clearly labeled as model projections, not market prices.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Groups and knockout bracket">
    Group tables track standings (played, won, drawn, lost, goal difference, points) alongside each team's chance of winning its group. The knockout view shows the bracket — round of 32 through the final — with slots projected from current group standings until the real draw is confirmed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Outright markets">
    Season-long markets that run across the whole tournament: **tournament winner**, **group winners**, and **player awards** such as top scorer and best player.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Teams and news">
    Team pages collect a team's info, markets, and coverage in one place. The news section aggregates World Cup coverage with social sentiment, and highlights which teams and players are generating the most buzz.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Analytics">
    Live market intelligence across the tournament: total volume, market counts, unique traders, and which markets are moving right now. The hub also compares Kash prices against other prediction platforms on the same fixtures, and pairs market odds with social sentiment data — a signal, not a prediction.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Leaderboard and prizes

The hub runs a tournament leaderboard during the World Cup: rankings are based on points earned from realized P&L on World Cup markets within the tournament window. The hub's **rules page** has the current prize tiers, the exact scoring formula, and eligibility terms (18+, void where prohibited).

There is also a free-play option — trade World Cup markets with play-money balance on Kash testnet and still climb the leaderboard.

# Suggest a market

See a question the community should be trading — a stat line, a milestone, a storyline? Use **Suggest a market** in the hub. Suggestions go to the moderation queue, and approved markets go live for everyone with credit to you on the hub.

<Tip>
During match days the hub is the fastest way to trade the tournament: live fixtures, moving markets, and community sentiment are all one tap from a trade.
</Tip>
