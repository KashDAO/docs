# Leaderboards

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where to find the leaderboards">
    **Community and Players Pages**

    - **[app.kash.bot/community](https://app.kash.bot/community):** community leaderboards and highlights
    - **[app.kash.bot/players](https://app.kash.bot/players):** browse and compare individual traders

    Every leaderboard entry links to a public profile, so you can see how top traders perform and follow the ones you rate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How rankings work">
    **Real Performance Metrics**

    Traders are ranked by verifiable on-platform performance:

    - **Realized P&L** — actual profit and loss from settled positions
    - **Volume** — total trading activity
    - **Win rate** — share of predictions that resolved in your favor
    - **Streak** — consecutive winning predictions

    Rankings update as markets resolve, and top gainers and losers are highlighted so you can see who's hot and who's not.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Competition Mode

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Competition points">
    **Compete for the Top of the Board**

    Competition mode scores your trading with **competition points**: trade during a competition and your performance earns points that determine your position on the competition leaderboard.

    - **Automatic participation** — trade during an active competition and you're in
    - **Transparent scoring** — points reflect your real trading performance
    - **Live standings** — watch your rank move as markets resolve
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Fair play">
    **Competitive Integrity**

    - Rankings are computed from on-chain trading activity — no self-reported results
    - Market manipulation and multi-account abuse are prohibited and can lead to disqualification
    - One account per person keeps competitions fair for everyone
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Achievements

Achievement badges recognize milestones across your Kash journey — trading activity, wins, streaks, and community participation. View your collection at [app.kash.bot/achievements](https://app.kash.bot/achievements), and show it off on your public profile.
