# Governance Today

<Note>
Today, Kash is operated by the Kash team. Protocol smart contracts are secured with multisig controls, and market resolution is executed by a multisig oracle. A progressive transition to community governance (KashDAO) is planned.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Current Governance Reality">
    **Kash-Operated with Multisig Security**

    - **Team-operated:** Product decisions, market curation, and protocol upgrades are made by the Kash team
    - **Multisig-secured contracts:** Privileged contract actions require multi-party approval rather than a single key
    - **Multisig oracle resolution:** Market outcomes are resolved through multisig-controlled oracle transactions
    - **Self-custodial funds:** Your funds sit in your own smart account — governance structure never gives Kash control over user funds

    **How to influence Kash today:**
    - Use the in-app feedback widget to report bugs and share ideas
    - Join the [Discord community](https://discord.com/invite/n9eumqjMPb) to discuss features and markets
    - Email support@kash.bot with product feedback
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Planned Transition to KashDAO">
    **Progressive Decentralization Roadmap**

    KashDAO is planned as a decentralized autonomous organization where $KASH token holders would collectively govern the protocol through transparent, on-chain voting.

    **Planned Architecture:**
    - **OpenZeppelin Governance** implementation for secure on-chain voting
    - **Token-holder voting** on protocol decisions
    - **Multisig emergency protocols** for crisis situations

    **Planned Governance Scope:**
    - Protocol parameter adjustments and fee structures
    - Treasury fund allocation and spending decisions
    - Oracle selection and data source improvements
    - Emergency protocols and crisis management procedures
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Planned Governance Components

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Treasury Management">
    **Community-Controlled Protocol Resources (Planned)**

    Under the proposed design, a governed treasury would collect protocol revenue and fund ecosystem growth:

    - **Treasury Core Module:** Would store USDC, $KASH, LP tokens, and sub-fund balances
    - **Asset Manager Module:** Would deploy idle USDC into pre-approved yield strategies
    - **Smart Burn Module:** Would execute monthly $KASH buybacks
    - **Community Rewards Module:** Would manage grants and community incentives

    **Planned Oversight:**
    - Community votes on treasury parameter adjustments
    - Transparent, on-chain reporting of treasury operations
    - Multisig security for large fund movements

    See [Tokenomics](/getting-started/ecosystem/tokenomics) for the full proposed treasury design.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Oracle & Dispute Resolution">
    **Ensuring Fair Market Outcomes**

    **Today:**
    - Markets are resolved by a Kash-operated multisig oracle
    - Multi-party approval is required before a resolution executes on-chain

    **Planned:**
    - DAO-driven oracle updates and data source improvements
    - Community oversight of resolution accuracy
    - Community-appointed adjudicators and weighted community votes for disputed or ambiguous outcomes
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Emergency Protocols">
    **Crisis Management & Security**

    **Today:**
    - Multisig controls (Gnosis Safe) protect privileged protocol actions
    - The team can pause affected components in response to a security incident

    **Planned:**
    - Governance-gated emergency multisig activation
    - Time-locked actions with mandatory community notification
    - Post-incident analysis shared with the community
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

# Planned Token-Based Voting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How Decisions Would Be Made">
    **Proposed Decision-Making Process:**
    - **Proposal submission** by community members
    - **Discussion and debate** in governance forums
    - **On-chain voting** with transparent results
    - **Implementation** based on community consensus

    **Proposed Voting Weight:**
    - Base voting power determined by $KASH holdings
    - Staking multipliers for locked tokens (see [Tokenomics](/getting-started/ecosystem/tokenomics))
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How to Prepare">
    **Get Involved Before the DAO Launches**

    - **Stay informed:** Follow [official channels](/getting-started/ecosystem/socials) for governance and token announcements
    - **Join the discussion:** The [Discord community](https://discord.com/invite/n9eumqjMPb) is where governance design will be discussed
    - **Build reputation:** Active, constructive community members will be best positioned to participate meaningfully

    <Warning>
    There is currently **no way to buy $KASH or acquire governance rights**. Anyone offering to sell you $KASH, an allocation, or voting power is running a scam.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
