# Referral Program

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How referrals work">
    **Invite Friends, Earn Together**

    1. Open **Portfolio → Referrals** on [app.kash.bot](https://app.kash.bot)
    2. Copy your personal referral link
    3. Share it with friends — when they sign up and trade, their activity counts toward your milestones

    **Milestones:**
    - Referral rewards unlock in milestone tiers as your invited friends become active
    - Track your progress, active referrals, and unlocked milestones directly on the Referrals page
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Affiliate program">
    **For Creators and Communities**

    If you run a community or audience, the affiliate program offers ongoing rewards for the users you bring to Kash — beyond the standard referral milestones.

    - Apply and manage everything from the Referrals page
    - Questions? Reach out to partnerships@kash.bot
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
