# Ready to Join?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Quick Start Checklist">
    **Get Connected in Minutes**

    1. **Create an account** at [app.kash.bot](https://app.kash.bot)
    2. **Follow [@kash_bot](https://x.com/kash_bot)** for markets and announcements
    3. **Join [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/n9eumqjMPb)** to connect with the community
    4. **Subscribe to [Telegram](https://t.me/kashDAO)** for instant updates

    **Pro Tips:**
    - Introduce yourself in Discord's welcome channel
    - Start with small predictions to learn the platform
    - Ask questions — the community loves helping newcomers
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Community Etiquette">
    **How to Be a Great Community Member**

    **Do:**
    - Share genuine insights and analysis
    - Help newcomers learn and succeed
    - Report bugs and suggest improvements — the in-app feedback widget goes straight to the team
    - Engage respectfully with different viewpoints

    **Don't:**
    - Spam or excessively self-promote
    - Share false or misleading information
    - Harass other members
    - Attempt market manipulation
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
