# Platform Availability and Compliance

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Kash available in my country?">
    **Availability and Compliance Restrictions**

    **Availability:**
    - Kash is available in supported regions. Access is blocked where law or policy requires — including sanctioned regions and other restricted jurisdictions.
    - Eligibility requirements, including the current list of restricted regions, are in the Terms of Service available in the app.

    **Restricted Activities:**
    - **Illegal markets:** no markets on illegal activities
    - **Age restrictions:** must be 18+ or the legal age in your jurisdiction
    - **AML compliance:** adherence to anti-money-laundering requirements

    **Support for Global Users:**
    - Interface available in English, Spanish, Portuguese, Russian, and Chinese
    - Markets display in your local time
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the age requirements?">
    **Age Requirements**

    - **Minimum age:** you must be 18 years or older to use Kash
    - **Local laws:** some jurisdictions may impose higher age requirements — the stricter rule applies
    - **Self-declaration:** by using the platform, you confirm you meet the age requirement

    Kash monitors for misuse and may restrict accounts that violate age requirements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to verify my identity?">
    **Identity Verification Requirements**

    **Basic use:**
    - No identity verification is required to create an account — sign in with X, Google, email, or a wallet
    - Only essential information is collected

    **When verification applies:**
    - Some fiat deposit/withdrawal providers require KYC for their services
    - Additional verification may be required by regulation in certain regions or for certain volumes
    - Requirements may change as regulations evolve

    **Data protection:**
    - Minimal data collection and storage
    - Clear disclosure of data practices
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Tax and Financial Responsibilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are my tax obligations?">
    **Tax Reporting and Compliance**

    **User responsibility:**
    - You are responsible for reporting gains and losses under your jurisdiction's tax laws
    - We recommend consulting a tax professional
    - Keep detailed records of all transactions

    **Available information:**
    - All transactions are recorded on the Base blockchain and verifiable on basescan.org
    - The Portfolio page shows your trade and claim history

    **Typical taxable events:**
    - Profits from winning predictions

    **Considerations:**
    - Whether earnings are treated as income or capital gains varies by jurisdiction
    - Losses may be deductible in some jurisdictions
    - Cross-border implications for international users
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Kash handle financial regulations?">
    **Regulatory Compliance Framework**

    **Current measures:**
    - **Geographic restrictions:** geo-blocking of OFAC-sanctioned regions
    - **AML monitoring:** anti-money-laundering screening measures
    - **Age requirements:** 18+ only
    - **Content moderation:** no markets on personal harm, death, or criminal outcomes

    **Data protection:**
    - Privacy considerations built into platform features
    - Data minimization — only necessary information is collected
    - Handling complies with applicable privacy laws

    **Ongoing development:**
    - Continuous monitoring of regulatory changes
    - Features adapted to maintain compliance as requirements evolve
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if regulations change?">
    **Regulatory Change Management**

    - **Monitoring:** continuous tracking of regulatory developments with expert consultation
    - **Communication:** advance notice of impactful changes when possible, in plain language
    - **Adaptation:** features or regional availability may be adjusted to maintain compliance
    - **Continuity:** service maintained wherever legally possible, with reasonable transition periods when features are restricted

    Because Kash is self-custodial, your funds remain in your own smart account regardless of platform-level changes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

# Community Guidelines and Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the community guidelines?">
    **Community Standards and Behavior**

    **Core principles:**
    - Treat all community members with respect
    - Engage honestly in prediction markets
    - Keep discussions relevant and appropriate

    **Prohibited activities:**
    - Market manipulation or fraudulent activity
    - Multiple accounts to circumvent limits
    - Harmful, offensive, or inappropriate content
    - Using the platform for any illegal purpose

    **Enforcement:**
    - Violations can lead to restrictions or account action
    - Report inappropriate behavior via the in-app feedback widget or support@kash.bot
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What about data privacy?">
    **Privacy and Data Protection**

    **Data collection:**
    - Minimal collection — only what's needed to provide the service
    - Login data comes from the provider you sign in with (X, Google, email, or wallet)
    - Transaction data is recorded on the public blockchain

    **Data usage:**
    - Used only for platform functionality
    - Personal data is not sold to third parties
    - Anonymous, aggregated analytics for platform improvement

    **Privacy considerations:**
    - **Public blockchain:** all on-chain trading activity is publicly visible
    - Your public profile is associated with your trading activity
    - Blockchain records are permanent and cannot be deleted
    - To delete your Kash account data, contact support@kash.bot — see the [Account FAQ](/getting-started/faqs/account)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
Legal and regulatory requirements vary by jurisdiction. You are responsible for ensuring your use of Kash complies with all applicable local laws and regulations.
</Warning>

<Tip>
All your prediction activity is recorded on the Base blockchain and is publicly visible. Keep detailed records for tax and legal compliance purposes.
</Tip>
