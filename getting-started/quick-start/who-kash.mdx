<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Traders">
    **Anyone with an opinion about a future event**

    - Trade multi-outcome markets on sports, crypto, politics, tech, and entertainment
    - Start with any amount — no minimum, no trading experience required
    - Track P&L in your [Portfolio](/getting-started/guides/winning), climb the [leaderboards](/getting-started/ecosystem/competitions-and-tournaments), and earn achievements
    - Optional extras: [parlays](/getting-started/features/parlays), tweet-trading on X, and the upcoming [Earn vault](/getting-started/features/earn)

    Sign-in takes seconds, gas is sponsored, and your funds stay in your own smart account.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Market creators">
    **People who spot questions worth trading**

    - Propose markets from the Create form: question, outcomes, resolve time
    - Pay a creation fee; markets go live after review
    - Well-made markets attract volume and community discussion

    See [Creating Opportunities](/getting-started/guides/creating-opportunities) for the full flow.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Organizations & partners">
    **Communities, media, and brands**

    - Create and curate markets for your audience
    - Embed markets and threads on your own site via iframes
    - Run a branded market page and earn revenue share on trades in markets you create

    Start at [For Businesses](/for-businesses/overview).
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Developers">
    **Builders integrating prediction markets**

    - Public REST API for market data, portfolios, and trading
    - Official CLI, TypeScript SDK, and Python SDK
    - Webhooks and embeds for real-time integrations

    Start at the [Developer Docs](/developer-docs/introduction).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What makes it accessible

- **No crypto expertise needed** — sign in with X, Google, or email; a wallet is created for you, and gas is sponsored
- **No minimums** — trade with whatever amount you're comfortable with
- **Self-custody** — funds live in your own smart account, not with Kash
- **Global, 24/7** — available worldwide, subject to regulatory restrictions

<Note>
Kash is operated by the Kash team today. A token and community governance are planned but have not launched — any references to $KASH or a DAO describe future plans, not the current product.
</Note>
